What counts

Only invested assets push you toward FIRE — not home equity unless you plan to sell. Your mortgage offsets real estate equity. Debts reduce net worth but don't generate FIRE income.

The standard approach: low-cost index funds, minimal fees, compound growth. Your FIRE number is a one-time calculation. Your progress updates as your portfolio grows.

How FIRE number is calculated

FIRE Number = Annual Expenses ÷ SWR. At 4% SWR, that's 25x your annual expenses. At 3.5% SWR (more conservative), that's about 28.5x.

This tracker is for educational purposes only. FIRE depends on taxes, Social Security, pensions, healthcare, and spending flexibility. Not financial advice.
