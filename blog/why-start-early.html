Why Start Your Coast FIRE Journey Early?

The surprising truth: starting early matters more than saving extra.

The Core Principle

Coast FIRE isn't about how much you save—it's about when you start. The earlier you begin, the less you need to save.

Why Time Beats Money

Starting at 30 vs. 40 with identical savings rates can mean a $2-3 million difference in your Coast FIRE number.

Here's why:

  • → Compound interest is exponential: Every dollar saved at age 30 has 10+ years to grow before you even start coasting
  • → Market returns work for you: A 7% annual return compounds differently than a flat 5% inflation hedge
  • → Lower savings burden: You can achieve Coast FIRE with $1.5M at 40 vs. $2.8M at 35 (same lifestyle)
